Programme Structure

June Workshops

Kickstart your coding journey with hands-on lessons and fun collaborative activities. You'll learn Python basics and complete a guided mini project.

Project Phase

Work in small teams to develop your own project with mentor support. You'll have regular check-ins and optional meetups.

Final Showcase

Present your project, celebrate your achievements, and see what others have created.
